About Us


Mount Everest was founded in Vienna in September 2006 as a destination management company and supplier for ground services in central Europe on B2B basis. Our service includes Hotel and Apartment reservations for individuals and groups, rent a car service, Airport service (meet and assist), shuttle service, day trips and sightseeing tours for individuals and groups, delegation service, travelling insurance and Visa for several countries, bookings of flight and train tickets, Cargo, private Jets and charter services.

We would like to advise you that we work with professional and qualified partners. In addition to that our company is GIATA member and fully certified travel agency and tour operator in Europe.

We assure you that we will provide you a high level of services which will be subject to your customer’s satisfaction as a result of our accuracy, honesty, punctuality and our reputation. This is why we stand as your destination management company
